ABOUT US

We are lovers of electronic music and we want to bring you all the local and international news of this environment. New events around the world, new releases, interviews and much more. The idea is to create a space for exchange with DJs and their audience, having live talks with them, offering workshops on how to produce and play music and also have a radio frequency where electronic music is playing 24/7, 365 days a year. We are a small group of clubbers born in Uruguay and we want to show the world the national scene in this environment. Thank you for visiting our site! Enjoy the trip.
